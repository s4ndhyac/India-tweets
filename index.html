How is America feeling right now?

This map shows how people are emotionally tweeting realtime. The colors indicates positive or negative feelings, and they are loosely analyzed by words used in tweets. The data comes from @PubNub public data network, which streams actual messages on Twitter at a maximum rate of 50 Tweets per second.

  • More positive tweets
  • Equal numbers of positive and negative tweets
  • More negative tweets
